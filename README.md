# Your Retro Mixtape

The app draws a random, curated music video based on the genre selected by the user. In addition to the video, the user receives background information or other interesting facts about it. The goal is to offer an alternative to traditional music video viewing, allowing users to discover unfamiliar videos while learning something new about songs they already know.
